MP1584EN Buck Converter (12V→5V)

Mount with tape on back of board. 4 wires:

  • IN+ → Row A col 8 (after diode) — 12V
  • IN− → Row B col 19 — GND
  • OUT+ → Row C col 5 — 5V
  • OUT− → Row B col 20 — GND

⚠ Set trimpot to 5V BEFORE connecting!

Power Protection (Row A–B)

  • D1 (1N4007): Anode col 2, cathode col 4. Band → RIGHT.
  • C1 (100µF 50V): + Row A col 6, − Row B col 6. Observe polarity!

Voltage Divider (Row K)

Signal(5V) → R1 → Tap(3.33V) → R2 → R3 → GND

  • R1: col 2 → col 6 (bridges cut at col 4)
  • R2: col 8 → col 11 (bridges cut at col 9)
  • R3: col 13 → col 16 (bridges cut at col 14)

Tap = 5V × 20K/30K = 3.33V ✓
